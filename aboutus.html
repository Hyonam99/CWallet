How C-wallet Works

C-wallet is the best place to fundraise, whether you are an individual,group or an organisation.

Find answers to your questions

  • Do Donors get Charged.
    No, never. Donors are charged only the amount they choose to donate to you and that’s it.
  • How fundraising work on C-wallet.
    The simple answer is: Share your campaign as far and wide as possible, because this is how people are going to find it. Social media is a great resource, so if you have Facebook, Instagram, or Twitter accounts, those would be great places to start. It’s also important to make sure that you have a compelling fundraiser story since that is what people will read to learn more about your goals.
  • Can I Raise Money For Anything.
    Almost. As always, common sense goes a long way here. DO raise money for personal causes that are purposeful, humanitarian cause and physiological cause. DON’T break any local laws, expect strangers to buy you stuff or promote inappropriate content.
  • How Will I Know If Someone Donate.
    You’ll receive an email notification each time a donation is made to your C-wallet campaign and it will be shown on your dashboard where you can track and monitor your campaign.
